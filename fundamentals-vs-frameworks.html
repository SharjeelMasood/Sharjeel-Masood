Why Learning Fundamentals Matters More Than Frameworks

Frameworks change every few years, but fundamentals stay forever. This is the difference between short-term success and a long-lasting career.

The Framework Trap

Many developers chase trending frameworks without understanding basics. This leads to shallow knowledge and frustration.

What Are Fundamentals?

Fundamentals include HTML structure, CSS layout principles, JavaScript logic, browser behavior, and problem-solving skills.

Why Companies Prefer Fundamentals

Companies want developers who can adapt. A developer with strong fundamentals can learn any framework quickly.

Long-Term Career Growth

Frameworks help you get interviews. Fundamentals help you get promotions, stability, and confidence.

Final Advice

Learn the basics deeply. Frameworks will follow naturally. This mindset separates professionals from beginners.
